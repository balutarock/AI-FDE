
 Job Description (API Developer)  
     1   Design, develop, and maintain RESTful and GraphQL APIs for scalable, secure, and high-performance applications.  
     2   Proficiency in front-end frameworks (React, Angular, or Vue.js) and responsive UI/UX design principles.  
     3   Strong back-end development experience with Node.js, Python, Java, or .NET frameworks.  
     4   Build and manage microservices architectures with containerization (Docker) and orchestration (Kubernetes).  
     5   Implement authentication and authorization frameworks (OAuth 2.0, JWT, SSO).  
     6   Optimize API performance through caching, load balancing, and rate limiting strategies.  
     7   Integrate APIs with third-party services and internal systems for seamless workflows.
